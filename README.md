# 🌿 Wildcat's Wonderful Weed List (WWWL)

> *Yo, welcome to the stash.* A personal cannabis strain directory with 300+ strains, interactive filtering, strain tracking, and a cyberpunk Matrix-green aesthetic — all running as a static GitHub Pages site. No login required, just vibes.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔍 Live Search
Type any strain name into the search bar and watch the results filter instantly with smooth fade animations. Multi-word searches work too — all words gotta match. If you come up empty, an animated "No strains found!" popup pops up with a lil' surprise sound effect. 🔊

### 🌿 Type Filtering (Legend Bar)
Click the sticky filter bar at the top to narrow down by strain type:
- 🔵 **Indica** — Blue cards for when you wanna chill on the couch
- 🔴 **Sativa** — Red cards for when you need that creative energy
- 🟢 **Hybrid** — Green cards for the best of both worlds
- 🟡 **Unknown** — Yellow cards for the mystery strains
- 🌈 **Show All** — Reset and see everything
- 🩵 **Tried** — Check out your personal collection

The legend hides when you scroll down (less clutter, more focus) and pops back up when you scroll back up. Smooth like that.

### ✅ Tried Strain Tracker
Each strain card has a clickable circle toggle. Mark strains you've tried — they glow up teal and bold. Your selections save automatically to `localStorage`, so they stick around between visits. No account needed, no hassle. Just click and remember.

### 🎲 Random Strain Picker
Can't decide what to try next? Hit the **"Pick random?"** button and let fate decide. It highlights a random visible strain with a glowing pulsing animation and scrolls it right into view. Perfect for when you're too baked to choose. 😎

### 💾 CSV Export
Click **"Save My Strains"** to download a `.csv` file of all your tried strains. The export includes the strain name, type (Indica/Sativa/Hybrid/Unknown), and direct links to Leafly/Allbud for each one. Backup your stash, share with friends, whatever you want.

### 📊 Live Strain Counts
The filter bar shows live counts next to each category label, updating in real time as you search or toggle tried strains. Always know how many strains you're working with.

### 💬 Community Chat
A Firebase-powered chat section at the bottom lets you vibe with other visitors. Styled to match the dark Matrix theme with frosted glass aesthetics. Drop a message, share your experiences, or just say hi. It's all anonymous and chill.

### 🎨 Cyberpunk / Matrix Theme
Built with a dark background, glowing green (`#00ff00`) accents, frosted-glass containers, and smooth CSS animations throughout. It's like browsing from inside the Matrix, but with more weed. Fully responsive for mobile and desktop — looks good on your phone while you're on the go.

## 📋 Strain Data
